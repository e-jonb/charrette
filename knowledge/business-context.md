# Business Context

> Deeper understanding of the business, team dynamics, and workflows that surfaces during architecture sessions but doesn't fit in the structured org profile. Newest entries at top.
>
> This file captures organizational insights that inform architecture decisions — things like how the business actually works, who uses what, why certain constraints exist, and what's really driving a project. The Chief Architect reads this at session start to apply context that isn't obvious from requirements alone.
